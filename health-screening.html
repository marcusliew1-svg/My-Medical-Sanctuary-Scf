Frequently Asked Questions

Simple answers before you book.

Do I need to fast?

Some screening components may require fasting. MMS will confirm your preparation instructions before your appointment.

How long does it take?

Timing depends on the screening scope. The team will advise the expected duration when your appointment is confirmed.

Can international patients book?

Yes. International patients can enquire and coordinate appointment timing with the MMS team before travelling.

Will I see a doctor?

Yes. Screening is designed around doctor review, so your results can support a personalised health plan.

Can Ling interpret my results?

No. Ling helps explain general health concepts but does not interpret individual medical results.
